Our Achievements
  • The release of the book " Merrily Crossing The Woods " by Hassan Suhail
    Venue: Inspiration Public School, Haldwani
    Event Date: 22-Feb-2023
    Description:

    The release of the book " Merrily Crossing The Woods " by Hassan Suhail a student of class 8 at Inspiration Public School Kathgodam was done by the chairperson of the  School, Dr. Geetica Balutia. On this occasion Principal Mr. Anurag Mathur, Hassan Suhail's parents Suhail Anwer Shamsi and Humaira Shamsi, brother Huzaifa Suhail, Umar Siddiqui, Mrs. Shazia Mudassir, Mrs. Rabia Javed, Mrs. Nazia Shamsi, Vice Principal of Inspiration public school, Mrs. Mamta Taneja and other teachers were present. After the inauguration of the book, Chairperson Dr Geetika Balutia, Principal Mr Anurag Mathur, Suhail Anwer Shamsi, Humaira Shamsi and Huzaifa Suhail put up their views and blessed the young author.
